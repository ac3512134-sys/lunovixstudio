Origin Story

Why Lunovix Studio Built This

Pomodo Focus was engineered because modern productivity tools are fundamentally broken. They're either bloated with features nobody uses, locked behind subscription walls, or secretly optimized to keep you inside the tool rather than doing actual work.

Version 7.0 introduces the fully-realized vision — Luna AI now interprets your natural language and controls the entire workspace. Say "I want to study physics for an hour" and she creates the mission, adds it to your queue, and asks if you want to start. No clicks required.

How It Works
  1. Open & Go Immediately
    No login required. The workspace is live the moment you arrive. Sign in only to save data and access reports.
  2. Talk to Luna AI
    Luna understands natural language. "Schedule a 90 min deep work block for coding" creates the task, sets the timer, and asks to start.
  3. Set Your Soundscape
    Six synthesized sound environments — all generated live via Web Audio API. Works completely offline.
  4. Engage & Flow
    Hit Engage. The clock moves, analytics update in real-time, and the bell chimes on completion.
  5. Review Your Weekly Report
    Every week, generate a professional PDF report with charts, insights, and personalised improvement suggestions.

FAQ
Is login required?
No. Full access without an account. Sign in only to persist stats, earn badges, appear on the leaderboard, and access weekly PDF reports.
How intelligent is Luna AI?
Luna uses the Anthropic Claude API for genuine intelligence — she understands natural language, interprets your intent, creates missions, controls the timer, switches sounds, and builds personalised schedules automatically.
How does the weekly report work?
Logged-in users can generate a PDF report from the Analytics panel at any time. It includes total focus hours, most productive days, streak performance, and personalised improvement suggestions.
Is my data private?
Yes. All data is stored in your browser's localStorage. Nothing is sent to any server. Your data never leaves your device.
