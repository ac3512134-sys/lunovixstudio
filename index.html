The complete operator guide for Pomodo Focus — AI-powered deep work engine for people who take their time seriously.

Origin Story

Why Lunovix Studio Built This

Pomodo Focus was engineered because modern productivity tools are fundamentally broken. They're either bloated with features nobody uses, locked behind subscription walls, or secretly optimized to keep you inside the tool rather than doing actual work.

Version 6.0 introduces the fully-realized vision — a three-panel neural workspace with Luna AI on the left, your focus engine in the center, and your soundscape and analytics on the right. Everything is accessible without navigation. Everything is live. No login required to start — just open and focus.

How It Works
  1. Open & Go
    No login required. Open the site and you're immediately in your workspace. Sign in optionally to save progress and compete on the leaderboard.
  2. Set Your Soundscape
    Pick a sound from the right panel — Quantum Rain, Lo-Fi Matrix, White Noise, Bio-Forest, Soft Piano, or the Interstellar organ theme. All synthesized live via Web Audio API.
  3. Consult Luna AI
    The left panel is your always-on AI co-pilot. Ask for schedules, motivation, time plans, or Pomodoro guidance. Luna adds tasks directly to your queue automatically.
  4. Engage Your Session
    Hit Engage on the center timer. Your analytics update in real-time in the right panel. The bell chimes when your cycle completes.
  5. Track Progress
    Your 7-day output map, streak, total minutes, and session count all live in the right analytics panel — always visible, always current.

FAQ
Is login required?
No. You can use the full app as a guest — timer, sounds, Luna AI, everything. Create an account only if you want to save your stats, earn badges, and appear on the leaderboard.
Is the Interstellar sound from the film?
No — it is an original synthesis inspired by Hans Zimmer's compositional language. It is not a reproduction of any copyrighted material. All audio is generated procedurally using Web Audio API oscillators.
How does the leaderboard work?
The leaderboard ranks all browser-stored accounts by total focus minutes. Demo competitors ensure it's never empty. A shared cloud leaderboard is on the Lunovix roadmap.
Is my data private?
Yes. All data is stored exclusively in your browser's localStorage. Nothing is sent to any server. Your data never leaves your device.
Is this free?
Completely free, forever. No subscriptions, paywalls, ads, or required accounts. Lunovix Studio believes great tools should be accessible to everyone.
