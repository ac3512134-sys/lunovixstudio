Origin Story

Why We Built This

Modern productivity tools are optimized to keep you inside the tool rather than doing actual work. Pomodo Focus v8.0 is different — Luna AI understands natural language and controls the entire workspace automatically. Study Together connects you with a focus partner in real-time. And everything works without ever requiring a login.

Version 8.0 introduces the most stable timer engine to date, a rebuilt Luna AI with genuine Claude intelligence, and Study Together focus rooms for social accountability.

How It Works
  1. Open & Go Immediately
    No login required. The full workspace is live the moment you arrive. Sign in only to persist stats and unlock weekly reports.
  2. Talk to Luna AI
    Luna understands natural language. "Study physics for 1 hour" creates the task, sets the timer, and asks to start. "I feel tired" gets a recovery suggestion.
  3. Study Together
    Create a focus room, share the code, and study in sync with a partner. Timer states stay coordinated across the session.
  4. Review Weekly Reports
    Every week, generate a professional PDF report with 7-day charts, streak data, and Luna's personalised improvement suggestions.

FAQ
Is login required?
No. Full access without an account. Sign in only to persist stats, access reports, and appear on the leaderboard.
How does Study Together work?
Create a room to get a code. Share it with a partner. Both users join the same room and can see each other's presence and sync timer states.
How intelligent is Luna AI?
Luna uses the Anthropic Claude API for genuine natural language intelligence. She interprets intent, creates missions, controls the timer, and provides personalised coaching based on your stats.
Is my data private?
Yes. All data is stored in your browser's localStorage. Nothing is sent to any server unless you explicitly use Luna AI features which call the Claude API.
